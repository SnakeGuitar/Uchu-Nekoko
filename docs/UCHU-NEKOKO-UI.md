# UI Specification: Uchu Nekoko Profile Card

## 1. General Layout & Theme
* **Background:** Solid dark charcoal gray.
* **Typography:** Sans-serif font family used throughout. Text colors contrast against the dark background using pure white for headings and light gray for body text.
* **Structure:** A vertical card layout divided into two main sections: a full-width top banner and a bottom content area split into two distinct columns (Releases on the left, Biography on the right).

## 2. Component Breakdown

### A. Header Banner (Hero Section)
* **Container:** Full-width rectangular image block at the top.
* **Image Description:** A muted, nostalgic anime-style illustration of a girl with brown hair holding an orange and white cat. They are looking over a townscape with a railway crossing, train tracks, and power lines in the background. 
* **Text Overlay (Top-Left):**
    * **Primary Title:** "Uchu Nekoko" (Large, bold, pure white).
    * **Subtitle:** "宇宙ネコ子" (Smaller, slightly translucent or light gray white, placed directly below the primary title).

### B. Main Content Area
Located directly below the banner, featuring a padding margin that separates the content from the edges of the card.

#### Left Column: "Releases" Section
* **Heading:** "Releases" (Medium size, bold, white, left-aligned).
* **Image Grid:** Two square images placed side-by-side with a small gap in between.
    * **Left Image (Thumbnail 1):** Album cover featuring an anime girl with short hair, wearing earphones, looking downwards/sideways. Color palette is light blue and grey.
    * **Right Image (Thumbnail 2):** Album cover featuring an anime girl with long dark hair, wearing a white t-shirt, looking directly at the viewer against a botanical/greenhouse background.
* **Call to Action (CTA) Button:**
    * **Shape:** Rounded rectangle button spanning the full width of the two images above it.
    * **Color:** Lighter gray background (contrasting with the dark charcoal main background).
    * **Label:** "View More" (Centered, pure white, regular weight).

#### Right Column: Biography Section
* **Positioning:** Placed to the right of the "Releases" section, top-aligned with the "Releases" heading.
* **Text Block:** A single paragraph of body text.
* **Typography:** Light gray color, regular weight, with comfortable line height for readability against the dark background.
* **Content:** "Uchuu Nekoko (Uchū Nekoko), also known as Universe Nekoko, is a Japanese dream pop duo from Kanagawa formed in 2012. It was formed by Nemuko Yamanaka and Kōhei Tanabe, but currently comprises Yamanaka and Kano. The duo have never released their faces, instead choosing to identify themselves through anime avatars."
